# Ideation Backlog

Ideas for improving the product. Score and prioritize before adding to sprints.

## Scoring (1-5 each)
- **Impact:** How much does this improve the user experience or business?
- **Effort:** How much work? (1 = huge effort, 5 = quick win)
- **Confidence:** How sure are we this is the right thing to build?

**Priority = Impact × Effort × Confidence** (higher = do first)

## Parked Ideas
Ideas we're not pursuing now but don't want to lose.

## Rejected Ideas
Ideas we evaluated and explicitly said no to, with reasoning.

---

## Ideation Process

### When to Ideate
- After every retro (what would have made this sprint better?)
- When user reports friction ("this is annoying" = idea signal)
- When analyzing competitors or similar products
- During dedicated ideation sessions (monthly)

### How to Evaluate
1. Write the idea in one sentence
2. Score Impact/Effort/Confidence
3. If score > 45: add to next sprint planning discussion
4. If score 25-45: keep in backlog, revisit monthly
5. If score < 25: park or reject with reasoning

### Rules
- No idea is stupid — capture first, evaluate second
- Rejected ideas get documented (avoid re-debating)
- Ideas from real user feedback always score +1 Impact
